ABOUT ME

I'm a perfectionist and an artist at heart, striving to bring both into whatever I do. I am a team player and go all in for my team! In the quest for my purpose in life, I realized I am innately passionate about all animals, and I hold wildlife and it's conservation above all else. In the long run, I intend to utilize my wealth towards the betterment of society and the planet as a whole.

I am also passionate about coding. During the three years' break from a two year job right out of college, I came to develop a fondness for the unique challenges of coding. And so my latest hobby is solving problems, which I get to do a lot, as a student at UNCC Coding Bootcamp.
